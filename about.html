About FOLK — Foundation For Living Knowledge

"Indigenous communities are pioneers of technologies that offer solutions to climate change."

Julia Watson

Who are we?

We are a non-governmental organization to uphold the values of indigenous practices, local knowledge, and cultural heritage for resilient societies and climate action.

What do we do?

We strive for community-led change among geographically marginalized and vulnerable communities and build resilient societies through channeling indigenous/local ecological knowledge.

Our Vision

Ushering in a community-led change among the geographically marginalized and vulnerable communities and building resilient societies through channelizing indigenous/local ecological knowledge.

Core Values

Local Knowledge
Empathy
Inclusion
Sustainability
