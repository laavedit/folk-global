Who We Are

Foundation For Living Knowledge

FOLK (Foundation For Living Knowledge) is a non-governmental organisation dedicated to disaster risk reduction, cultural heritage protection, and the preservation of indigenous knowledge systems across India and South Asia.

We work in the Himalayan regions of Himachal Pradesh and beyond, collaborating with communities, district administrations, and international partners to document, preserve, and apply traditional knowledge in the face of modern climate and disaster challenges.

Our operational headquarters are in Gandhinagar, Kullu, Himachal Pradesh — at the heart of the communities we serve — with our registered office in New Delhi.

Our Values

What We Stand For

🌱

Indigenous First

We centre traditional and local knowledge in every project, treating communities as experts of their own landscapes and heritage.

🤝

Community Partnership

Our work is done with communities, not for them. Genuine collaboration and consent are non-negotiable.

📖

Documentation & Memory

Living knowledge dies without documentation. We record oral traditions, craft practices, and ecological wisdom before they are lost.

🌏

Climate Justice

The communities most threatened by climate change are often the custodians of the knowledge needed to survive it.

⚖️

Equity & Inclusion

We work across gender, caste, and economic lines to ensure that resilience is built for every member of a community.

🔬

Evidence & Rigor

Combining traditional knowledge with modern science — GIS mapping, disaster management frameworks, and academic research.
